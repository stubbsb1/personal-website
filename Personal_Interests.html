My Personal Interests.

Northern Kentucky University Student

Working on Cars

My journey as a car enthusiast has been a thrilling ride, filled with unforgettable experiences and a deep-rooted passion for all things automotive. It all started at a young age, captivated by the sleek lines and powerful engines of the cars I saw on the road. I found myself admiring cars throughout the entirety of my chilhood/teenage years.

But it wasn't just about admiring cars from a distance. I wanted to get my hands dirty, to understand the inner workings of these machines that ignited my imagination. So, I dove headfirst into learning about car mechanics, devouring books and online tutorials, and seeking out mentors who could teach me the ropes.

My thirst for knowledge and hands-on experience led me to take a bold step after graduating high school—I embarked on a journey to Texas to purchase a 2016 Mustang GT. It wasn't just about buying a car; it was about diving into the world of automotive craftsmanship with both feet.

From that moment on, the Mustang became more than just a vehicle—it was a project, a challenge, and an opportunity to learn and grow. I took on the task of doing all the work on the car myself, immersing myself in every aspect of its maintenance, repair, and customization.

PC Hardware / Buidling

My fascination with PC building and hardware is just one facet of my deep-seated passion for technology, a passion that has been a driving force in my decision to pursue Computer Information Technology..

Beyond assembling computers, I thoroughly enjoy delving into networking technology, including routers, switches, firewalls and other networking equipment. There's something incredibly satisfying about configuring and optimizing these systems to create efficient and reliable networks.

Whether it's troubleshooting connectivity issues or fine-tuning network performance, working on computers and networking technology is not just a hobby—it's a thrilling adventure that I'm eager to explore further in my studies and career.
